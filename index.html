About me

Hello, I am Rafael, an enthusiast of math and its applications. Since young I nourish a love for mathematics. But it was only when I read the book "Real Analysis", from Elon Lages Lima, that I was able to spot the real beauty of it, hidden in its formalism. Math most of the times can be chalenging and frustrating but certainly, I mean, almost certainly, it is the most fascinating thing invented by mankind ;)

Studying math and programing are my passion - as well as sharing it, and that's why I have created an account on github. So, whenever possible, I will be sharing my work here. But, bare in mind that I will be using this platform more as a place where I can keep track of my studies and programming codes. That being said don't expect fantastic Readmes all over the places. Although I will push myself to keep everything well documented.

Recently, the areas that interest me most are topological data analysis, machine learning and discrete simulations. At the section of Topics you can find out more about me and what I am working on.

Whenever I come up with an interesting idea I will be writing about it and posting here. It can be a proof of a Theorem, a piece of code, or some tips on programming, ... The important, as life has taught me, is that knowledge must be free and shared.
